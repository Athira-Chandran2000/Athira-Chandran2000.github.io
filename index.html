Research & Projects

Cross-Lingual Speaker Verification for Indic Languages

Developed a robust cross-lingual speaker verification system by pre-training on high-resource English (LibriSpeech) and fine-tuning on low-resource Indic languages such as Tamil and Malayalam. Implemented an ECAPA-Conformer architecture with multi-head attention to fuse spectral and prosodic features. Enhanced embedding discriminability using hybrid AAM-Softmax and Triplet loss, combined with extensive data augmentation.

Multilingual Hate Speech Detection using ML and Transformers

Designed a multilingual hate speech detection pipeline for English and Malayalam social media datasets. Compared traditional machine learning models, CNN–BiLSTM architectures, and transformer-based models including BERT, MuRIL, mBERT, and IndicBERT. Addressed class imbalance and optimized hyperparameters for low-resource NLP settings.

Neonatal HIE Segmentation and Classification Framework

Proposed a two-stage framework combining deep learning–based medical image segmentation and machine learning classification. Achieved a Dice score of 0.749 and classification accuracy of 89%, with improved consistency across severity grades. Presented at IEEE INDICON 2025.
